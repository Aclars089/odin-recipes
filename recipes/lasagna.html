Lasagna

Mama Spinelli's Homemade Lasagna

This Recipe was handed down for five generations. It contains five layers of delicious pasta, cheeses, meats, and sauces. This dish is the culmination of a lifetime of trial and error resulting in literally the best lasagna in history.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Mozerella Cheese
  • Spiced Dog Meat
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Ricatta Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat 3/4 of the tomato sauce for 5 minutes then add dog meat, let stand for 2 minutes.
  2. Add remaining 1/4 sauce as bottom layer of sauce to pan
  3. Add layer of lasagna noodles
  4. Add meat sauce
  5. Add ricatta cheese
  6. Create second layer substituting ricatta for parmisean.
  7. Repeat step 6 topping with mozeralla
  8. Bake at 400 for 15 minutes or until cheese is slightly browned
