Bubba's Greasy Burger

This Recipe was handed down for five generations. It contains five layers of spices. This dish is the culmination of a lifetime of trial and error resulting in literally the best burger in history.

Ingredients

  • Grease
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • American Cheese
  • Dog Meat
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Pickles
  • Bubba's 5 layer Buger Spice
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Buns

Steps

  1. Add 5-layer spice into dog meat, mix
  2. Heat 3/4 of the grease for 5 minutes then add dog meat.
  3. Cook meat until outside brown, inside pink to 165 degrees internal temp
  4. Add remaining grease
  5. Once outside is crisped, add cheese
  6. Place on bun and desired topping order
